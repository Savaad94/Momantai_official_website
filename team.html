The heart and soul behind every creation.

Chef Jack Chan

Executive Chef

Born in Hong Kong, Jack Chan discovered his passion for cooking in his family’s restaurant. He trained at renowned culinary schools in Hong Kong and Paris, mastering classical French techniques while embracing his Cantonese roots. Over a 25-year career, he worked in Michelin-starred kitchens, including The Peninsula Hong Kong, Le Meurice in Paris, and Eleven Madison Park in New York, refining his ability to blend tradition with innovation.

As Executive Chef at Momantai in Granada, Jack seamlessly combines Andalusian ingredients like saffron, olive oil, and Iberian ham with the bold, vibrant flavors of Asian cuisine. His menus are a testament to his creativity, offering guests an unforgettable fusion of East and West.

Jack’s leadership has elevated Momantai to a destination for fine dining in Granada. Collaborating with local farmers and suppliers, he ensures every dish reflects sustainability, seasonality, and exceptional quality. When not in the kitchen, Jack explores Granada’s historic streets and markets, drawing inspiration from the city’s rich cultural tapestry.

Thibault Chiumenti

Principal Sous Chef

Hailing from Burgundy, France, Thibault Chiumenti combines the elegance of French gastronomy with the precision of Japanese cuisine. Trained at the Institut Paul Bocuse and shaped by Michelin-starred kitchens and a transformative journey through Japan, he brings a refined yet innovative touch to every dish.
As Sous Chef at Momantai, Thibault's commitment to harmony and sustainability shines through his creations, blending tradition and modernity to craft unforgettable culinary experiences that embody serenity in every flavor.

Oscar Chow

Chef de Partie Saucier

Hailing from the bustling culinary streets of Hong Kong, Oscar Chow is Momantai’s Chef de Partie Saucier, specializing in hot kitchen cuisine. With over a decade of experience, Oscar has mastered the art of wok cooking and sauce preparation, blending the vibrant flavors of Cantonese traditions with modern techniques.
At Momantai, Oscar’s fiery expertise brings dishes to life, delivering bold, harmonious flavors that elevate each meal into an unforgettable culinary journey.

Shelby Anderson

Chef de Partie Garde-Manger

Shelby Anderson, Momantai’s Cheffe de Partie Garde-Manger, is a maestro of cold preparations. Originally from Seattle, she draws inspiration from the crisp flavors of the Pacific Northwest and her training in contemporary European techniques.
Her creations balance artistry and precision, showcasing the finest seasonal ingredients in every dish. At Momantai, Shelby transforms simplicity into sophistication, crafting refreshing appetizers and salads that embody elegance and harmony.

Adam Pfeiffer

Chef de Partie Pâtissier

Adam Pfeiffer, Momantai’s Chef de Partie Pâtissier, brings his passion for pastry to life with every creation. Hailing from Vienna, Austria, he is deeply inspired by the city’s rich dessert traditions and his years of experience in Michelin-starred patisseries across Europe.
At Momantai, Adam crafts exquisite pastries and desserts, blending classic techniques with modern artistry. Each plate is a celebration of flavor and texture, leaving diners with a sweet and lasting impression of culinary perfection.
